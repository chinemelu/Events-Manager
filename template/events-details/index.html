Description

The Office of Research and Graduate Studies is in the process of assessing the impact of its current Strategic Research Plan, and is looking for further direction on its implementation during its final two years. It is our goal to include all key stakeholder groups, beginning with early career, mid-career and full professors. If you fall into one of these three groups you’re invited to participate in the upcoming Strategic Consultation Dialogues.

Dialogue IV: Monday, Nov. 20, 3pm – 5pm in the CAC 130 (The Alpine Room) Graduate Studies program coordinators, 3pm – 4pm; graduate students, 4pm – 5pm

Food and drink will be provided and no RSVP is required. You are welcome to come for all or part of the sessions. Bring your ideas and questions, engage in the structured dialogue, and ensure that your voice is heard as we discuss priorities.
We look forward to working together during this strategic review process.
